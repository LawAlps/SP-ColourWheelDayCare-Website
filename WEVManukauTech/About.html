Our Beliefs

"Whāngaia ka tupu, ka puāwai."

"That which is nurtured, grows and blossoms."

We offer your children a secure and dynamic educational environment.

Our one of a kind conditions are outfitted with excellent common play areas where one may discover logs, rocks, timber playhouses and even a dinghy for our youthful innovative children! We have separate zones devoted to every one of our valuable age groups, where they will encounter carefully considered qualified, committed and submitted educators offering a scope of learning opportunities in a semi-organized program.

Colour Wheel Daycare isn't only a childcare centre, it is a network, a family and an extensionn to your family. We are very strong advocates for family connections and our teaching tean are devoted to your desires and needs for your child.

Who are we?

Colour Wheel Daycare is a 100% Kiwi owned and operated home-based childcare provider that is licenced by the ministry of education. Unlike childcare centres, our maximum ratio is one Educator to four children (including a maximum of two under two's).

We are an emerging daycare that has two branches and planning to expand. The primary goal of Colour Wheel Daycare is to provide the highest childcare service throughout South and East Auckland at a competitive price for the best and most caring service for your children.
